use wasd to control player 1 (white)
use arrow keys to control player 2 (black)
player 1 press space to select a block, then use wasd to swap with the adjacent blocks
player 2 press L to select a block, then use arrow keys to swap with the adjacent blocks
press G to generate the puzzle
